WSIB Lab - Website Redesign

Overview – Redesign of the WSIB Innovation Lab website

Role – Designer along with 1 other designer and 1 developer

Tools – Sketch

Context

There was a need to redefine the target audience for this website and design accordingly. Prior to this redesign, the website was used as a starting project for each group of students. It was decided that this would be the last iteration.
The Lab was also undergoing a change in branding and the website needed to reflect this update.

Goal

Develop the final design for the Lab website to incorporate a new colour palette and logo.
Improve the design to account for the newly defined audience.

Target Users

The target users are students, specifically those interested in working at the Lab.

Research

Short interviews were conducted with the current students at the Lab to obtain pain points, areas of improvement, and sections that were desired.

Takeaways

  • Include more details of projects that the Lab was working on
  • Improve the formatting of the blog page
  • Include more details on the Lab itself

Previous Version

Most of the website’s content was presented directly in the home page with a full continuous scroll. There were separate pages for the blog posts and projects.

Design

New Version

The main page now acts as a very long navigation bar, showing previews of each section (about, projects, team, and blog). Each project is now spread out on a full page, including details about the process, team members, tools used, goals, and conclusions. There is a full page dedicated on describing what the Lab is about. The layout for the team and blog pages were also reorganized.
